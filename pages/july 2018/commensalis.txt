content:

Commensali are complex, dynamic light transportation networks buried deep within the matrices of thriving Metropoles. Through their increasing size, some Commensali have developed into self-sufficient, pulsing, and breathing systems. Although disquieting for some, these Metropoles now rely on the energy delivered by their Commensalis for their continued survival.
